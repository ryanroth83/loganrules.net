How to install Doom on Ti 83/84 Plus

This Tutorial is Very Simple to Follow

  • Step 1
    Download the Doom.8xp and mirage os file from here and here.
  • Step 2
    Download Ti Connect CE from here.
  • Step 3
    Connect your calculator to your computer with the USB cable provided with your calculator.
  • Step 4
    Open Ti Connect CE and select the calculator icon on the left side.
  • Step 5
    Drag and drop the Doom.8xp and the MirrageOS.8xk file onto your calculator in the TI Connect CE App.
  • Step 6
    Open the MirageOS app on your calculator and select the Doom app to play.
